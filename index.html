SMART · Instagram Integration Demo

This page demonstrates the end-to-end Instagram authorization flow used by SMART. Meta login and permission approval are real. Instagram content shown below is mocked solely for Meta App Review.

Step 1 — Connect with Facebook (Meta OAuth)

The user authenticates with Meta and explicitly grants SMART read-only permissions.

Permissions requested:
• pages_show_list — list Pages the user manages
• pages_read_engagement — confirm Page ↔ Instagram connection
• instagram_basic — read public Instagram posts

SMART cannot post content, read messages, manage ads, or access private data.

Step 2 — Select Instagram Account

After permission approval, SMART retrieves the user's Facebook Pages and linked Instagram Business / Creator accounts using pages_show_list.
